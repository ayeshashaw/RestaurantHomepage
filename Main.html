About Us

The history of restaurants dates back to ancient Rome and China, where establishments offered food and drink for travelers. In the 18th century, Paris saw the emergence of the first modern-style restaurants, serving a variety of dishes in public dining spaces. The concept spread to Europe and the United States in the 19th century, evolving from inns and taverns. Fast food chains like McDonald's revolutionized dining in the 20th century, while the 21st century brought about culinary innovation, fusion cuisine, and an emphasis on sustainability. Today, restaurants are integral to global culture, offering diverse culinary experiences and playing a significant role in social gatherings.
